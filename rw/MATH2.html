Kugabanya(Imibare) - IchigoJam

Ni iyihe mibare igabanyika 182?
Itegeko "FOR" cyangwa "NEXT", Mugihe wongereye ububiko X bukava kuri 1 ivuye kuri 2 hanyuma ukabisubiramo.
Igisubizo ni OK igihe "N=182" ukayishyira "LET N, 182"
Leka turebe "ikigabanywa", umubare turibubone.
Uragabanyika neza cyane!
Ni uwuhe mubare ushobora kugabanya 455? What is the number that can divide 455?
Turabona umubare ushobora kugabanya icyarimwe 182 hamwe 455
7,13 na 91 niyo mibare igabanya 182 hamwe na 455.
Umubare munini ushobora kugabanya 182 na 455 ni 91.
Gerageza gukora ibi!
1. Garagaza imibare "igabanya byombi" 3432 na 2002
2. Gabanya 3432 n'umubare "munini ushobora kubigabanya byombi"
3. Igisubizo ubonye kuri kabiri(2) gikubeho "umubare munini ubigabanya byombi"(3432 na 2002)
4. Noneho bara imibare ishobora "kugabanya" 2002
5. Reba umubare "wa mbere" udafite iikigabanya mu mibarwa ine(4)
6. Reka Twemezeko ntamubare ugabanya hose uri hagati y'imibare yambere
