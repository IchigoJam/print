Kwibuka imibare / LET - IchigoJam

Mudasobwa ifite ubushobozi bwogufata mumutwe burenze ubundi bwose, Ishobora kwibuka imibare yose ibitse muri A kugeza kuri Z!
Iyo yerekanye "OK", Iba irangije kubika byose(iba irangije gufata mu mutwe)!
Zimya sikirini(Insakaza mashusho), hanyuma werekane A? B? C?
Ikoresha "LET" kugira ngo ukerekane aho ugiye kubika, hanyuma ukandika icyo ubikamo.
Ishobora nogukoresha inyuguti aho kwandika imibare.
Ikicyimenyetse "="(Bihwanye) gishobora kwifashishwa tushaka kwerekanako bingana.
Ijwi rigenda buhoro!
Gerageza gukora ibi:
1. Kora ijwi rigenda buhoro cyane
2. Kora ijwi ryo hejoru cyane(ryihuta)
3. Ibuka umwaka wavutsemo, ukwezi, umunsi hanyuma ubike iyo mibare yose
4. Reka twibuke kandi tugaragaze umubare wakubwe ku kwezi n'umusi wavutsemo
5. Gerageza Ni imibarwa ingahe ushobora kwibukamo
