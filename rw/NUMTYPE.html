Wabonye iryo wakoresheje?
Hindura umukino w'inyuguti ube ABC!
Ese ushobora kuwuhagarika mu gihe kingana na masogonda rya 20?
Hindura umukino ube umukino wo kwandika imibare!
Ese ushobora kuwuhagarika mu gihe kingana na masogonda rya 10?
Ni gute amanota yagaragara hejuru y'imubare y'ibice?
Hashize isegonda rimwe nyuma yakitso, rangiza umukino.
Gerageza gukora ibi:
1. Hindura umukino wokwandika amagambo uko ushaka
2. Iga ijambo rikomeye ryo mucyongereza wifashishije umukino wo kwandika
3. Hindura umukino ube uwo kwandika imibare ya zeru na rimwe
4. Hindura umukino ube uwo kwandika inyuguti ushetseiyo ari yo yose
5. Hindur umukino ube uwo kwandika emogi
