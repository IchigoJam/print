Touch Typing

Touch typing to hit without looking at the keyboard is fast! fun! cool!
Position the index finger of the right hand at "J" position, middle finger at "K", ring finger at "L".
Type quickly J, K or L that displays randomly.
Can you hit 10 times without mistakes? The target time is 8 seconds!
After you cleared it, let's add the alphabet characters gradually.
Position the index finger of the left hand at "F".
Can you give out 8-second record with "FJKL"?
The middle finger of the left hand at "D", the ring finger at "S" and the little finger at "A".
This is the home position of the touch typing!
Try it!
1. Let's give out 8-second record with "ASDFJKL"
2. Let's add "H" that press with the index finger of the right hand
3. Let's add "G" that press with the index finger of the left hand
4. Let's add "ZXCVBNM" located at bottom of keyboard
5. Let's add "QWERTYUIOP" located at top of keyboard
6. Let's give out 8-second record with all alphabet
