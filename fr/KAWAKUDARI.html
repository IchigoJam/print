"Kawakudari Game" is a game that avoid obstacles and go down the river.
First, decide your own character.

Enemy character, appearance.
※ For 「(」「)」, hold down Shift key and press 「8」「9」 respectively.

Too fast? Stop with ESCAPE key, and insert "WAIT".
To be able to move with the left and/or right key.
Attach the hit judgment for the completion of the game!
